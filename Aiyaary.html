Aiyaary Movie details

Aiyaary (English: Ultimate Trickery) is a 2018 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film[2] written and directed by Neeraj Pandey.[3] [4] Produced by Shital Bhatia, Dhaval Gada & Motion Picture Capital and distributed by Reliance Entertainment, Plan C Studios, Friday Filmworks & Pen India Limited. The film features Sidharth Malhotra, Manoj Bajpayee, Rakul Preet Singh, Pooja Chopra, Adil Hussain, Kumud Mishra, Naseeruddin Shah and Anupam Kher in lead roles.
Development
The film was announced in April 2017. It was shot in Delhi, London, Kashmir, Cairo and more. Aiyaary is directed by Neeraj Pandey.
Release
The film was originally scheduled to be released on 26 January 2018 but this was moved in order to avoid clash with Padmaavat. The film is now scheduled to be released worldwide on 9 February 2018.[6].But now the release date is further postponed to 16 February 2018.
