Welcome to New York Details


Welcome To New York is an Indian 3D Comedy film, directed by Chakri Toleti, produced by Vashu Bhagnani and Jackky Bhagnani, and starring Diljit Dosanjh, Sonakshi Sinha, Karan Johar, Riteish Deshmukh, Lara Dutta and Boman Irani. It is set to be released worldwide on 23 February 2018.
Plot
Teji (Diljit Dosanjh), a sloppy recovery agent, and Jeenat Patel (Sonakshi Sinha), a fashion designer, become part of a big Bollywood event in New York - and chaos ensues.
